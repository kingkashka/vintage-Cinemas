Pulp Fiction

A darkly comedic crime film that intertwines multiple stories involving a pair of hitmen, a boxer on the run, a gangster and his wife, and a pair of diner robbers. Through its nonlinear narrative, the film explores themes of violence, redemption, and the unpredictable nature of life.

Inglorious Basterds

A revisionist World War II film that follows two parallel plots: a group of Jewish-American soldiers known as the "Basterds" who are on a mission to terrorize the Nazis, and a young French cinema owner who plots to avenge her family's murder by the Nazis. Both plans culminate in a daring attempt to assassinate key Nazi leaders, including Hitler, during a film premiere in occupied France.

Kill Bill

A visually striking revenge film that follows "The Bride," a former assassin who wakes up from a four-year coma after being betrayed by her team on her wedding day. She embarks on a relentless quest to hunt down and kill the members of her former assassination squad, leading to a showdown with O-Ren Ishii, the first on her hit list. The film is known for its intense action sequences, including a climactic battle with the Crazy 88, and its homage to martial arts cinema.

Kill Bill 2

The conclusion of "The Bride's" revenge journey, this film delves deeper into her past, revealing the reasons behind her betrayal. The Bride continues her quest to eliminate the remaining members of her former assassination squad, including the final showdown with her former lover and mentor, Bill. Unlike the action-heavy first volume, Volume 2 focuses more on character development, dialogue, and the emotional resolution of The Bride's story.

Show Times

The Hateful Eight

A tense and claustrophobic Western set in post-Civil War Wyoming, where eight strangers, including a bounty hunter, a fugitive, and a former Confederate general, seek shelter in a remote cabin during a blizzard. As they wait out the storm, mistrust and hidden agendas emerge, leading to a deadly game of deception and violence. The film is known for its intense dialogue, slow-building suspense, and dramatic twists.
